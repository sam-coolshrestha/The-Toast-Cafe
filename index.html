Our Story

A cafe born from
Agra's own hunger.

The Toast Cafe didn't start with a business plan. It started with a craving — for a place in this city that felt like yours. Somewhere to sit with your laptop and a brown butter latte on a slow Tuesday morning, or pull a chair with friends over a wood-fired pizza on Friday night.

We set up shop at Padam Highstreet, Tajganj, tucked into the heart of a neighborhood that already had character. We just gave it a kitchen, a coffee bar, and a reason to stay a little longer.

Every item on our menu is built around comfort — the kind that doesn't need to announce itself. Freshly baked breads every morning, handcrafted mocktails, and an espresso program we're quietly obsessed with.

The Coffee

We source single-origin beans and dial in our espresso daily. Our baristas have a standing obsession with getting the extraction just right — and it shows in every cup. Ask about the barista special.

The Food

Comfort food made properly. Our Detroit-style thin crust pizzas, loaded sliders, and signature Chilli Cheese Toast are the kind of things you'll be thinking about on the way home. Maybe even the next morning.

The Bakes

Our in-house bakery runs every morning. Focaccia that hits right, croissants with the correct amount of butter, and cakes that people have driven across the city for. Fresh, daily, never frozen.
